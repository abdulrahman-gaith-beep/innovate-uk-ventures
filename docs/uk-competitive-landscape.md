# UK Competitive Landscape

## Net Zero / Energy

| UK Player | What They Do | Weakness / White Space |
|---|---|---|
| Octopus Energy / Kraken | Energy retail platform with smart tariffs and flexibility | Consumer-focused; limited C&I flexibility aggregation |
| Habitat Energy | AI for battery storage optimisation and trading | Focused on utility-scale batteries, not distributed C&I |
| Electron | Flexibility market platform (FlexiGrid) | Infrastructure layer, not end-to-end aggregator |
| Kiwi Power (Engie) | Demand response and virtual power plant | Acquired by Engie; limited innovation; legacy architecture |
| Energy Systems Catapult | R&D on energy system modelling | Research focus; not commercialising SME-facing products |
| IONATE | Smart transformer hardware-software | Hardware-centric; not pure-play AI flexibility |

**White space:** AI-native platform combining building energy management, EV charging, battery storage, and grid service revenue stacking for UK C&I customers. No player integrates the full stack with real-time NESO/DSO market participation.

## Next-Gen Digital / Fintech

| UK Player | What They Do | Weakness / White Space |
|---|---|---|
| Archax | FCA-regulated digital securities exchange | Exchange/custody only; no portfolio management |
| Globacap | Primary issuance platform for digital securities | Issuance-focused; no integrated asset management |
| FNZ | Wealth platform infrastructure | Traditional fund admin; not tokenisation-native |
| Canoe Intelligence (US) | Alternative investment document automation | US-centric; no UK equivalent with FCA compliance |
| Addepar (US) | Multi-asset portfolio analytics | US-dominated; limited UK regulatory integration |

**White space:** UK-native AI-powered infrastructure connecting family offices/PE to tokenised AND traditional alternative assets, with FCA compliance, PISCES integration, and automated NAV/reporting under the new AIFM framework.

## Aquaculture / Food

| UK Player | What They Do | Weakness / White Space |
|---|---|---|
| Ace Aquatec | AI cameras for cage-based salmon farming | Cage-focused; limited RAS capability |
| Observe Technologies | Data analytics for salmon farms | Open-water focus; not RAS-optimised |
